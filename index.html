    Good to know

    • Nothing is uploaded anywhere. Parsing and spreadsheet generation happen locally in your browser tab using JavaScript; the files never touch a server.
    • Office codes are cost-centers, not postings. One office code can pay staff across many physical schools — treat it as a disbursing office, not a 1:1 school identifier.
    • "Est. Date of Entry in Service" (bulk mode) is an approximation, derived from the printed length-of-service — typically accurate to within a few days. Individual slips carry the exact date instead.
    • Bank Account No. is a raw account number, not a formatted IBAN.
    • Very large registers (100MB+, tens of thousands of rows) are normal for district-wide files — extraction may take a minute or two; the log on the right shows progress.
